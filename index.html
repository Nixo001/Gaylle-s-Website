My Mission Beyond the Stage

As Miss Tuburan Tourism 2024, my advocacy focuses on empowering youth through education. Growing up, I saw firsthand the challenges faced by students in our community, which inspired me to take action. Through my platform, I've organized educational workshops, partnered with local schools, and launched mentorship programs to support students.

One of my proudest achievements is establishing a scholarship fund that has helped over 50 underprivileged students. I also work with local organizations to promote environmental awareness and sustainable practices.
